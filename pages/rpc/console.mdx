# Enabling Console Rpc

<div className="steps-container">

### Open Kizzy
Open the Kizzy app on your device.

### Go to Console Rpc
Click on the `Console Rpc` card.

### Select a Template
You will see a list of game templates to choose from. Select the one you wish to use.

### Enable Console Rpc
Click on the `Enable Console Rpc` button to set that game details as your activity status on Discord.

### All Done!
Tha Console Game will now be displayed on Discord.

</div>
