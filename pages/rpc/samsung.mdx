# Samsung Rpc

### What is Samsung Rpc?

Samsung Rpc is a feature in Kizzy that shows `Playing on Samsung Galaxy`.

<Callout type="warning">
This Rpc is only available for members of our Discord server. If you are not a member, the Samsung Rpc will not be shown to you. [Join Now](https://discord.gg/vUPc7zzpV5) to access this feature.
</Callout>

### Here's how it works:
<div className="steps-container">

### Enable Your Apps
Enable the games you want to show in your Discord status by going to the Apps Rpc and turning on the corresponding switches.

### Turn on the Rpc
Enable Samsung Rpc in by turning on the switch in home screen.

### Test a Game
Open a game that you have enabled in Apps Rpc.

</div>
